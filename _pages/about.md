I am a Master's student in Artificial Intelligence at [Beijing Institute of Technology](https://www.bit.edu.cn/) (985), supervised by Prof. Chengpu Yu. Previously, I obtained my M.Sc. in Computer Science from [City University of Hong Kong](https://www.cityu.edu.hk/) (QS Top 50) and B.Eng. in Automation from [Beijing University of Technology](https://www.bjut.edu.cn/) (211, Top 10%).

My research focuses on **Embodied AI** and **Vision-Language Navigation (VLN)**, with the goal of enabling robots to understand natural language instructions and autonomously navigate in complex environments. I am particularly interested in:

- **Vision-Language Navigation**: Developing VLN systems that integrate VLM/LLM for semantic understanding and autonomous exploration
- **Multi-Agent Systems**: Building collaborative agent systems for research automation and task planning
- **Semantic Mapping**: Constructing semantic occupancy maps for robot perception and decision-making

I am currently working on InfoNav, a unified value framework integrating semantics and information gain for embodied navigation, which is under review at **IROS 2026**.

**I am actively looking for PhD opportunities in Embodied AI, Robotics, and Vision-Language Navigation starting Fall 2027.**
